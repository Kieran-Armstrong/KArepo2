Apps so far:

Replace char - Replaces characters with their numerical counterparts e.g. "i" becomes 1, "e" becomes 3

Calculator - Simple calculator app

Code Breaker - Shift cypher --- enter a code to be encrypted, then decrypt our code

Heads or Tails - A simple coin flip game, where you play against the computer to guess heads or tails. The final score will be displayed at the end to let you know who won.

QR generator - Enter a web URL to generate it's QR code, save and scan the QR code to get to the web page
